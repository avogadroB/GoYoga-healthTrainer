About

Yoga is an ancient practice that originated in India thousands of years ago. It is a holistic approach to health and well-being that integrates physical postures, breathing exercises, meditation, and ethical principles.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Strengthens muscles and enhances posture
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Boosts mental clarity and concentration
  • Promotes relaxation and better sleep

Gesture Based Yoga Training

Our website provides a unique approach to Yoga training. The integrated gesture recognition technology provides the user with the correct methodology and accurately monitors the user's position. It can be accessed anytime anywhere by any device providing complete personalized yoga practice.
